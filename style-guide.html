Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to build revenue and essentially be the main front of the business. The website is to allow consumers to create and design their own phone cases and then their designs are sent from that website to the warehouse to create their case and send it to them.

The website allows us to receive their information on what they want and where they are so that we can ship the product to them. It also allows us to receive funds for the case making process. The purpose of the website is to be the middleman from the company to the consumer.

Audience Statement

The audience for this website will be anyone who has a phone and is looking for a phone case that meets their protective and stylized needs. The main audience range is most likely going to be from ages 12 â€“ 40. The buyer is most likely gong to be somewhat financially stable because this is an accessory to a phone and is not essential. This means that the target audience will be in more wealthy cities and towns.

The people we hope to find are those who have had struggles with phone cases in the past. Either that they brake or that the design they saw was not exactly what they wanted. This website allows those people in that range to design and create their own case. The goal is to create options for hundreds of different phones makes and brands. My intention is for this website to be accessed on a laptop or pc.

Misson Statement

Our goal is to help those who wish to have a better phone case that they can make themselves, and to meet the costumers needs in what they desire stylistically, protectively or both. This allows them to feel secure about how their phone case looks as well as how protected their phone will be.

At first, my goal is to simply make presets of different phone case options for their specific phone and then to enable them to change colors. Eventually I would like to develop as simplistic a software as I can, that allows consumers to totally design their phone case from scratch. The website is to simplistically create a way for consumers to choose their own designs for their case and make it their own.
