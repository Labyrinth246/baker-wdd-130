Getting Started!

Welcome to the Get Started page! Here is where you will be designing your own phone case. Don't worry, the design process for getting your own phone case is very easy. We have strived to make it as easy as possible for you to design your own personal case for your own personal phone!

First things first, you choose your phone type. Currently we have about 8 phone types to choose from but we are constantly striving to include more for your needs. After your phone type has been chosen, there are two case styles you can choose from. Protective Style and Creative Style. Each case style strives to protect your phone and make it look good but the Protective case has less customizibility and the Creative case has more customizibility. The Protective case has more protection for your phone but less customizibility. After you have chosen your case style, you can choose the color you want! There is a main color and a highlights color to compliment the main color. You can switch between colors to find ones you like best. Once you have a color you like, you can either save your design with your account or submit the case design and purchase it!
